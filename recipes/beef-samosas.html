Beef-samosas Ingredients

Steps to make beef-samosas

  1. Bring a medium saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil. Stir in potatoes and peas; cook until potatoes are tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  3. Stir in garlic and ginger. Season with salt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, cinnamon, cardamom, and black pepper.
  4. Heat 1 quart of oil in a deep saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Mix cilantro and green chile peppers into potato and beef mixture. Place approximately 1 tablespoon of the mixture onto each phyllo sheet.
